Drill Rapper BDC_King Faces Backlash After Bragging on IG Live

March 1, 2025 – Station 9 KSENT | By: [Reporter Name]

In a shocking turn of events, local drill rapper Zachariah, known by his stage name BDC_King, has found himself under intense scrutiny after a controversial IG Live session where he bragged about a violent act. The rapper, known for his hard-hitting drill lyrics, posted a live stream in which he claimed responsibility for a killing, while flexing about his street credibility. The live session quickly went viral, raising both eyebrows and concerns among fans, local residents, and law enforcement.

In the IG Live, Zachariah boasted about his actions, seemingly unphased by the implications of his words. "Caught a body, didn’t wanna do it, but that’s the way it goes," he said, followed by a series of cryptic statements hinting at his involvement in a deadly confrontation. While he didn’t name names, the streets quickly made their own assumptions. Many speculate that the incident he alluded to may be tied to the recent death of a Demon gang member, known to be a rival to BDC.

The rapper’s comment that “the gun jammed, and I still made it happen,” only fueled the rumors, as it suggested a close encounter with the law and a possibly botched attempt at self-defense. Despite his nonchalant demeanor during the IG Live, sources close to the situation confirm that law enforcement is currently investigating the rapper for potential involvement in the shooting.

While some fans argue that Zachariah was simply engaging in typical drill rap bravado, others condemned his actions as a dangerous influence on youth. Authorities are reportedly watching closely as the investigation continues to unfold.
